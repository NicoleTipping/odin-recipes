Tortellini Soup

Description

Hearty, creamy, and packed with bold flavor, this vegetarian tortellini soup is the ultimate cold-weather comfort food. Made with spicy Impossible sausage, tender cheese tortellini, chickpeas, and a medley of fresh vegetables—carrots, celery, onion, and leafy kale—it’s a satisfying, one-pot meal that warms you from the inside out. Simmered in a rich veggie broth and finished with a splash of creamy coconut milk, each spoonful is comforting with a subtle kick of heat. Topped with a sprinkle of Parmesan, this soup is the perfect balance of spicy, savory, and soul-soothing—ideal for cozy nights and second helpings.

Ingredients

  • Yellow Onion
  • 3 Celery Stalks
  • 3 Large Carrots
  • 7.4oz Spicy Ground Impossible Sausage
  • 64oz Vegetable Broth
  • 13.5oz Full Fat Coconut Milk
  • 1 Stalk of Kale
  • 20oz Cheese Tortellini
  • 2tbs Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Dice onion, celery, and carrots then place in pot with olive oil and cook until vegetables are tender
  2. Add in Impossible Sausage and cook until browned
  3. Pour in Vegetable Broth and mix
  4. Partially cover pot with lid and let simmer for 30 minutes
  5. While soup is simmering chop kale into bite sized pieces
  6. After the soup simmers for 30 minutes, add the Tortellini and cook based on package instructions
  7. When there are only 5 minutes left on tortellini, add kale and coconut milk
  8. Top individual soup bowls with parmesan cheese
