Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food with this hearty vegetarian casserole, packed with tender broccoli florets, golden baked cheese, and savory Quorn Chiqin pieces. The Quorn adds a satisfying, protein-rich bite, making this dish both wholesome and deeply comforting. Perfect as a main course or a side, this casserole brings the cozy flavors of home-cooked goodness to your table—no meat needed.

Ingredients

  • 12oz Quorn Diced Chiqin Pieces
  • 2 cups Frozen Broccoli
  • 22.6oz Cream of Mushroom Soup
  • 1 cup Milk
  • 2 cups uncooked rice
  • 2 cups Shredded Sharp Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook rice per package instructions
  2. Once rice is cooked preheat oven to 425°F
  3. Combine Chiqin, Broccoli, Soup, Milk and Rice into a large bowl
  4. Once mixed poure into a 9x13 casserole dish and use a spatula to spread evenly
  5. Top with shredded cheese
  6. Cover with foil and bake for 45 minutes, then remove foil and bake for another 10 minutes
