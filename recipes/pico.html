Pico

Description

This vibrant, chunky salsa is a fresh and zesty staple that brings bold flavor to any dish. Made with ripe tomatoes, crisp red onion, jalapeño, and a splash of lime juice, pico de gallo delivers a perfect balance of brightness, heat, and crunch. It’s naturally vegan, gluten-free, and refreshingly simple—just chop, mix, and serve. Whether you spoon it over tacos, scoop it up with chips, or pair it with a rich casserole, this fresh pico adds a burst of color and tangy freshness to every bite.

Ingredients

  • 6 Large Roma Tomatoes
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 1 Jalapeño
  • Pinch of Sugar
  • Pinch of Salt
  • Juice from 2 Limes

Steps

  1. Dice the tomatoes, onion, and jalapeno and add it to a bowl
  2. Add the lime, sugar, and salt
  3. Taste with your favorite chip and add more of any ingredient based on taste
